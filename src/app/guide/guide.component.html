EVOLVE (L5)

EVOLVE, the Centre for Design Thinking & Innovation, launched in June 2015 was the first innovation lab in APAC.

It serves as the headquarters for APAC and focuses on enhancing client experience, generating insights using analytics, raising client advisor productivity, and creating products to meet the evolving needs of our wealth management clients in Asia Pacific and globally.

The Peranakan-themed Employee Café which features Swiss-styled timber beams reflects the diversity of our cultures through the universal language of food.

A variety of healthy food, local favorites and different cuisines is available for employees.

There is also a dedicated barista bar which serves coffee for a good cause. Every Wednesday, revenue generated from its daily sales will be donated to Club Rainbow, a charitable organization that places strong emphasis on the development journey of children.

Cyber Fusion Centre (L5)

Client floor Art Tour (L6)
Client floor Art Tour (L6)

At 9 Penang Road, we have carefully planned a client journey that will bring the best of UBS to our clients. Starting from their arrival at the concierge where our dedicated client service associates will seamlessly escort clients to our client floor to meet their CAs. Our client floor showcases reputable art pieces, a total of 22 varying types of meeting and dining rooms and elegant client lounge areas.

UBS has been an active contemporary art collector since the 1960s, the vast majority of these works are displayed in UBS offices globally. Artworks from 1974 to today are on display in UBS Singapore office, it showcases diverse mediums, reflecting UBS’s strong passion for contemporary art. Similarly, the collection actively lends works to major art museums and cultural institutions for public exhibitions.

UBS University (L4)
UBS University (L4)

The UBS University in Singapore is APAC’s regional headquarters for learning and development for our employees in this region. Previously housed at the Command House, it now occupies an entire floor at 9 Penang Road, a reflection of our commitment to talent development.

  • The new state-of-the art campus offers an in-house space with advanced technology to help us drive a continuous learning culture
  • The move to house UBS University under the same roof offers greater convenience and accessibility to our employees
  • Featuring flexible and fluid spaces – there are two multi-function rooms with collapsible divisors that can accommodate large scale events, creating a more interactive and engaging learning environment
  • One of the main highlights is the Lego wall with chalk board walls. This space allows employees to use the colorful Lego bricks and chalk markers for team brainstorming and ideation, letting your creativity run wild
  • Within the space, there is also a TEDx Screen that features an array of curated content that provides an alternative medium for learning
